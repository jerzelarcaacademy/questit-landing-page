for an Online Merchant

A Writing Quest · Open for Claiming

The Quest Brief

Be it known to all Hunters of the Guild that one Maria Santos, a verified Merchant of the QuestIt realm, hath posted this quest in need of skilled wordsmiths.

The Merchant operates a small shop of handmade jewelry and accessories and requireth five (5) compelling product descriptions crafted in the English tongue — each of eighty to one hundred and twenty words in length. The tone shall be warm, aspirational, and approachable.

The Merchant shall provide all product names, likenesses, and details. The Hunter shall provide the craft. No artificial or hollow prose shall be accepted — only copy that speaks as a true admirer of the goods.
